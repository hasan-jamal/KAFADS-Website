B2B & B2G program

The special KAFADS B2B & B2G Program is designed for both local and international startups looking to maximize their potential by providing them with a set of A-Z Entrepreneurship training, coaching, and mentoring. In this program, you will have the ability to reach more clients as we focus on results rather than just information. This program is special for the network of entrepreneurs, subject-matter experts, investors, and potential clients in Saudi and the GCC.
