About Kafads

“We help international companies who are not looking to expand at the moment to find partners and clients via our B2B programs.“ We at KAFADS Consultancy help companies establish themselves in Saudi Arabia quickly and efficiently in a reasonable price and transparent work as well as helping companies on B2B & B2G marketing, advertising, and PR in Saudi Arabia. We are a team of Saudi and international professionals coming together to provide a new perspective to the modern way of marketing and establishing a company without a hustle. Our expertise ranges from legal, academia, entrepreneurship with a combined experience of 40+ years. We aim at providing a more comprehensive view to investors entering the market and helping them in marketing, advertising, HR, Legal and PR.

establishment establishment program

This program provides the companies a wide door into the targeted market with many possibilities and opportunities. We help you get your MISA license, CR, Iqama (Saudi Residency), Work Permit, Visas, Medical Insurance, Bank Account, Writing POA, Writing AOA (MOA), Editing AOA when needed, register at Chamber of Commerce, Register at Qiwa, Mudad, Etimad, Saudi Business Center, Muqeem, Balady (Municipality), Modon (if needed), Office Space, Hiring the right talent, and other entities needed for SAR 60,000 only! During our Establishment Programs, we help our clients to understand the culture, dynamics, and possibilities of selling and increasing their chances of closing the deals.
